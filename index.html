What is CPU ?

The CPU, or Central Processing Unit, is the main component in a computer that serves as the brain of the system. The CPU is responsible for executing instructions and processing data. It performs arithmetic, logical operations, and controls other hardware. In a computer, the CPU interacts with memory, storage, and other devices to run programs and applications. In general, the faster and more powerful the CPU, the better the performance of the computer. Now there are two companies that dominate the laptop/computer processor market in the world, namely INTEL and AMD.

What is GPU ?

A GPU (Graphics Processing Unit) is an electronic component designed to process and render graphics or images on a computer. GPUs are often referred to as graphics processors because their main job is to produce visual displays that users can see, such as images, videos, and other graphical elements on a computer screen. Currently, the company that provides the most advanced GPU is NVIDIA.

More about gpu

Nvidia Graphics Processing Unit

NVIDIA is a technology company known for its hardware and software products in the field of computer graphics. Founded in 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem, NVIDIA initially focused on developing graphics chips for personal computers. Today, the company is well-known as a global leader in the development of GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) and various related technologies, such as artificial intelligence (AI), parallel computing, and visualization.
